## 🎬 What Are Comments?

In **Python**, comments are lines ignored by the interpreter.  
They are meant for **humans to understand your code**, not for execution.

🧠 Comments make your code easier to:
- Read 👀  
- Debug 🧩  
- Maintain 🛠️  

---
